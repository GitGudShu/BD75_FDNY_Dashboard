## Emergency Incident Classifications

* **Medical Emergencies**: Legitimate calls for service where an individual requires immediate clinical intervention or transport due to illness or injury.
* **Medical MFAs (Malicious False Alarms)**: Medical calls placed with the intent to deceive, such as a prank call for an ambulance where no patient exists.
* **NonMedical Emergencies**: Service calls that require an emergency response but are not health-related, such as gas leaks, downed power lines, or elevator rescues.
* **NonMedical MFAs**: False reports of non-medical hazards (e.g., reporting a gas leak that doesn't exist) or automated system malfunctions not involving fire.
* **NonStructural Fires**: Fires that occur in areas other than buildings, such as brush fires, vehicle fires, rubbish/dumpster fires, or wildland incidents.
* **Structural Fires**: Fires occurring within a residential, commercial, or industrial building that threaten the integrity of the architecture.
